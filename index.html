The American Academy of Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation (AAPM&R) is working towards a BOLD future by highlighting and supporting innovative and influential physiatrists across the country to achieve a new vision and opportunities for PM&R. This new vision was developed based on input from thousands of physiatrists.

Physiatry's Bold, New Vision

Physiatrists are

the essential medical experts in value-based evaluation, diagnosis, and management of neuromusculoskeletal and disabling conditions.

Physiatrists are

indispensable leaders in directing rehabilitation and recovery, and in preventing injury and disease.

Physiatrists are

vital in optimizing outcomes and function early and throughout the continuum of patient care.

Innovators and Influencers is your Academy's way of highlighting the physiatrists across our diverse specialty that are driving positive change.

Fully launching in 2018, we will be putting the spotlight on members and their practices as well as sharing ways for ALL members to lead in new innovative and influential ways.

Why

Beginning in 2016, AAPM&R launched an initiative to hear from members and define a new vision for the specialty, Visioning PM&R Bold. Through this initiative, we gained input from thousands of physiatrists across the country, many of whom have a passion for innovation in the specialty. In the spirit of those we met, both in person and virtually, we want to connect members with these stories and foster membersâ€™ journeys to be innovative and influential in their work and lives. Borrowing from Visioning PM&R Bold, this initiative is For Our Patients, For Our Specialty, and For YOUR Future.

Starting in 2018

We will be launching an all new website to feature members across the specialty including:

  • those who are pioneering transitions and putting together new plans for how to practice to achieve the specialty's vision
  • those who are taking on leadership positions to effect change
  • those who are impacting the future of our specialty

And we will be asking YOU to share your own story of what you're doing to be innovative and influential as well as providing new opportunities for physiatrists to grow these skills.

Innovators and Influencers will be celebrated for leading the way for the other physiatrists.

Who are Innovators and Influencers?

In short, any physiatrist. Navigating a transition in your practice, guiding the next generation, sharing in a critical conversation that impacts the future of the specialty? We want to hear your story! In addition, to help members further develop their innovation and influencing skills, your Academy is focused on creating resources and programs to support you. There will be more leadership development, more knowledge sharing, and more opportunities to grow your skills and be innovators and influencers of physical medicine and rehabilitation.
